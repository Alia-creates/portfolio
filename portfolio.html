Weather Application

Purpose of this project was to research live weather reports while also showcasing temperatures of 7 days. The instructor presented a source that would enable live feedback of the weather. Additionally, the goal was to display the day, cloud coverage, and wind speed. Students were instructed to structure the layout to their liking but also meet all requirements of the assignment.

World Clock Application

The world clock application utilized previous methods within the weather applcation. This application was structured using a provided source that would display live feedback of the areas around the world. The goal was to ensure the students Time Zone would align directly above the selected location around the world. However, unlike the weather app that would display any location, the world clock only listed locations that were selected during the coding process.
