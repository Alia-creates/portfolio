ABOUT ME

Alia is a dedicated and ambitious individual who strives to acccmplish more. Alia graduated Sacramento State in California with a Bachelors Degree of Science (BS) in offcupation safety and health (OSH). She proceed down her career for 5 years but ultimately felt the change of heart to continue to learn. With so many resources readily available, Alia took it upon herself to obtain a certificate in Google for Ecommerce and Marketing, Program Manager, and finally with, Coding. While she had no prior experience with coding, she felt compelled to learn a new world that continues to grow and evolve. Today you are witnessing her work and knowledge she has obtained through SheCodes.

With many more young women entering this field, we are proud to see an increase in diversity through knowledge.
